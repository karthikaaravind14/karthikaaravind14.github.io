About me

Hey There! I am Karthika Aravind

I did my graduation in Computer Science and Engg from College of Engineering, Chengannur, Kerala in 2014, after which I joined Evaidya Pvt Ltd, a health care firm based in Hyderabad, as a Front End Developer. I got a fair exposure here in web development, as I was actively involved in UI changes in the official company and kiosk websites. I then moved to TCS, where I got trained in Java and UI development. Currently I am employed at Zycus Infotech Pvt Ltd, in Bangalore. The company provides procurement performance solutions, and I am working on a project which involves the complete redesign and technology revamp of the UI of all of these solutions from scratch. I have also completed a Web Development and Designing Certification course from Internet Academy, a Japan based training institute. On a personal note, I am basically from Kerala, but currently residing at Bangalore. Dancing has always been my passion, and I always make it a point to never miss a chance to dance. When I am not working or dancing, I often like to explore my artistic side, by drawing, painting or creating some DIY crafts. I have also lately developed a keen eye for mobile photography.
